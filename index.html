Xinyang Song

I am currently a four-year Ph.D. student at School of Artificial Intelligence, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and also the NLPR, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), supervised by Prof. Zhenan Sun. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree in Automation from Nanjing University in 2022, advised by Prof. Huaxiong Li.

My research interests revolve around unified generation and understanding and controllable vision generation(image&video). If you are interested in my work, please feel free to reach out for discussions or collaborations!

Publications

Xinyang Song, Libin Wang, Weining Wang, Shaozhen Liu, Dandan Zheng, Jingdong Chen, Qi Li, Zhenan Sun
arXiv, 2025
